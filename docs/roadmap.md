## Phases relative to T (exam window)

### T-6 to T-4 months: Foundation and content coverage
- Finalize navigation and onboarding improvements.
- Ensure core catalog coverage for priority subjects.
- Stabilize quiz and exam simulation flows.
- Ship baseline analytics and progress tracking.

### T-4 to T-2 months: Guided practice
- Release revision planner MVP and study goals.
- Add mastery map and spaced repetition queue.
- Introduce core gamification (points, badges, streaks).
- Improve search and discovery with topic coverage.

### T-2 to T-0 months: Exam sprint
- Add simulation history and performance summaries.
- Optimize low-bandwidth and offline support.
- Enable reminders, streak recovery, and challenge modes.
- Add community Q&A for last-minute clarification.

### T+0 to T+2 months: Post-exam and retention
- Provide result reflection and improvement reports.
- Collect feedback on content quality and usefulness.
- Refresh content for next cohort and adjust difficulty.

### Off-cycle (between cohorts)
- Scale to new countries and series.
- Expand member tools and review workflows.
- Add monetization and partner integrations.

## Release waves (by role)

### Student
- Wave 1: Core learning and progress.
- Wave 2: Planner, mastery, and gamification.
- Wave 3: Community, notifications, and offline.

### Member
- Wave 1: Content creation and publish workflow.
- Wave 2: Review checklist and analytics.
- Wave 3: Reputation and collaboration features.

### Admin
- Wave 1: Configuration and user management.
- Wave 2: Moderation and analytics.
- Wave 3: Policy automation and audit trails.
